Honorian AskBot Logo Generator

This page displays the AskBot logo for Pampanga State University's Honorian AskBot. To save the logo:

  1. Take a screenshot of the logo above
  2. Crop it to include only the circular logo
  3. Save it as "askbot_logo.png" in your project directory

Alternatively, you can right-click the logo and select "Save Image As" in some browsers.
